🚀 We just built an AI-powered hiring system at the INDIA RUNS hackathon — and it changes how companies find talent.

Traditional hiring relies on keyword matching. Our system goes deeper: it uses semantic embeddings to understand what a candidate actually brings, not just buzzwords on a resume.

In 27 days, our team built a full-stack platform that:
🔹 Generates vector embeddings from resumes and JDs
🔹 Scores candidates across 4 dimensions — semantic fit, experience depth, behavioral signals, and contextual relevance
🔹 Ranks 500+ candidates in seconds with explainable AI scores

The result? Hiring managers get a ranked shortlist with clear reasoning — no more guesswork, no more bias from keyword filters.

Built with FastAPI, React, FAISS, MongoDB, and GPT-4o.

Proud of what our team shipped. The future of hiring is intelligent, not just automated. 💡
